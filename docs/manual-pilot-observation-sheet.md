## What To Watch For

- Did staff know which bill was selected?
- Did staff understand that tapping an available PS5/Pool adds it to the current bill?
- Did staff confuse "Move game" with adding another game?
- Did staff stop all games before checkout?
- Did payment amount and tender selection feel obvious?
- Did the printed receipt/invoice look usable?
- Did shift close explain open tabs or unusual actions clearly?
- Did reports match the paper/manual record?
